Posting service in telegram. The functionality of creating private links.

We faced a task of creating a publishing-service that would differ from other platforms by having its own functionality that preserves the protection of intellectual property. In other words, the inability to share private links and copy texts, posted by the author on a website.
This service is fully integrated into telegram-bot.
At first, the author links his bot to the website. Then he posts an article and pastes its link at the right stage of the funnel and an authorized user gets full access to the materials. An unauthorized user is not allowed by the service and could be redirected to pay for the access.

That’s how an article looks like for an authorized user. We used the corporate identity and the client’s design.

Here is a window appearing for an unauthorized user, who, for instance, got the link from someone having the access to the bot.

Our client, a nutritionist @katarina_forma, uses this service to post private intellectual information about health. And that’s how a link to an article could look like in the telegram bot.
